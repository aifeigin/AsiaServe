IT Business Constellations...

Why constellations in IT and business?

To see the real causes of problems that are not visible in analytics, data, or logic — and to make decisions that truly change outcomes: revenue, growth, and stability in business or career.

What is an IT constellation?

Nearly 30 years in IT — as an entrepreneur, co-founder, and consultant — have given me a deep understanding of how the industry really works: products, teams, money, growth, and failures.

In my work as a constellations facilitator, I see that many key limitations in IT are not technical or strategic — they are systemic and hidden. This is where constellations provide fast access to causes that cannot be seen through analytics or traditional consulting.

This approach is especially effective in IT: product launch and scaling, revenue growth, breaking career plateaus, job search, salary increase, team management, and personal positioning in the industry.
