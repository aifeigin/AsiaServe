What Types of Сonstellations Exist?
Business Сonstellation
Examines the business from the unconscious perspective of the client to gather more information and make the best possible decision.
Systemic Approach
Views the business organization as a system and focuses on transforming the organization to achieve optimal results.
Ancestral Сonstellation
We often unconsciously repeat the fates of our grandparents, even if we have never known them. It's crucial to recognize and resolve this to live our own lives.
Family Сonstellation
Much of who we are comes from our parents, but we can choose to see the world through our own perspective, not their beliefs or pain.
Trauma Сonstellation
Certain recurring events in adult life may originate from childhood. We revisit those moments to release potential energy for personal growth.
Spiritual Сonstellation
Here, we explore the soul's tasks, purpose, lessons, karmic stories, relationships, and catalysts (karmic agreements).
