3. Subject of the Agreement
3.1. The Facilitator undertakes to provide the Client with constellation services (hereinafter referred to as the “Services”) in an individual format.

4. Terms of Service
4.1. Duration of a session: up to 2 (two) hours.
4.2. Cost of a session: €70 (seventy euros).
4.3. Before the constellation session begins, a brief interview is conducted where the Client formulates the intended request, and the Facilitator confirms the feasibility of the service.
4.4. The Client’s request may be refined or adjusted after the interview but should remain aligned with the initially stated topic.
4.5. Payment is made after the interview but before the constellation session starts.
4.6. If the Client fails to attend the session without valid reason, the payment is non-refundable. However, the session can be rescheduled if the Client notifies the Provider at least 24 hours in advance of the agreed time.
4.7. Services are provided in Russian (preferably) or English. Use of other languages is possible only with the assistance of a translator, whose services are paid for by the Client.
5. Effective Date and Term of the Agreement
5.1. Effective date: The agreement becomes effective upon receiving written confirmation from the Client regarding the interview date.
5.2. Term of the agreement: The agreement remains in effect until the constellation session is conducted unless terminated earlier under the terms of this Agreement.
5.3. Termination of the agreement:
Termination of the agreement is possible:
- by mutual consent of the parties during the interview stage;
- if payment for the constellation session is not made at least 24 hours before its scheduled start;
- if the Client fails to attend the session without notifying the Provider.
6. Payment Terms
6.1. Payment is made in euros to the Facilitator’s account, based on an invoice (Fatura) issued to the Client after the interview.
6.2. Upon completion of the session, the Facilitator issues a receipt (Recibo) confirming the full provision of services.
6.3. If the Client breaches payment terms, the Facilitator reserves the right to refuse to provide services.
6.4. Refund policy: Payment is refunded only if the session was canceled for a valid reason confirmed by the Client, provided the Provider is notified at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.

7.1. The Facilitator is responsible for the conscientious performance of their obligations.
7.2. The Client assumes full responsibility for their actions and decisions before and after the session, as well as for the interpretation and use of the information received during the session.

8.1. The Facilitator does not guarantee specific results or changes in the Client’s business, personal, or family systems.
8.2. The effectiveness of constellations depends on numerous factors, including the Client’s personal circumstances and actions taken after the session.

9.1. The Facilitator undertakes to maintain the confidentiality of all information received from the Client during the provision of services.
9.2. The Facilitator processes the Client’s personal data in compliance with applicable laws, including the *GDPR* (General Data Protection Regulation).
9.3. Personal data includes the Client’s name, contact details, the request discussed during the interview, and other information provided during service delivery.
9.4. The Client has the right to request the deletion of their personal data or restrict its processing by contacting the Facilitator using the provided contact details.
10. Final Provisions
10.1. All disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved through negotiations. If a resolution cannot be reached, the parties may seek recourse in a court at the Provider’s location.
10.2. Services agreed upon and paid for before amendments to the Offer are provided under the terms valid at the time of payment.
By making payment, the Client agrees to the terms of this public offer.
